The gap

Good learning materials exist. Access doesn't.

Coaching institutes and paid platforms have multiplied — but so has the distance between a student's pincode and quality guidance. GyanSetu exists to shrink that distance to a single link.

1 in 3

students from low-income households drop out before completing secondary school, often over the cost of tuition or materials.

70%

of government-school students report never having used a structured online learning platform, despite most owning a basic smartphone.

₹15k+

is the average yearly cost of private coaching for board exams — out of reach for most first-generation learners.
